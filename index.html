About Us

Round One Boxing Gym is all about bringing that classic boxing spirit into a modern space. We're inspired by the legends — Ali, Foreman, Sugar Ray — and we carry that same energy into every session. Whether you're here to learn the basics, sharpen your skills, or just get a solid workout, we've got your back. No egos, no gimmicks — just real training, real results, and a community that pushes each other to be better. It doesn't matter where you start. What matters is that you showed up for Round One.

At Round One, you're learning from the best. Our coaching staff includes retired pro boxers and experienced trainers who've worked with athletes at every level — from day-one beginners to national champions. They bring real fight experience, proven techniques, and a no-BS approach to training. Whether you're chasing gold or just trying to level up, you'll be in the hands of people who know what it takes — because they've lived it.
